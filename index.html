A little about RoboPrix

“Every great journey begins with a small step”
A small step by us, the Robotics club of VIT Chennai towards a great journey –RoboPrix’19. Our very first, yet successful roboprix'19 , took place on 11th and 12th of February. It was a concatenation of challenging competitions which aimed at testing the hardware, designing, efficiency, accuracy and functioning of the competing robots. We were honoured to have had enthusiastic participants from various schools and colleges, in and around Chennai. The event witnessed a total of 312 zestful participants, 59teams. The vibrant event started on 11th February at 10pm with a grand inauguration. We were esteemed to have Mr. Ganesh Mani, senior Vice President of Productions at Hyundai Motor India Ltd as Chief guest. Our revered coordinator, Dr Arockia Selvakumar sir welcomed the inquisitive gathering. This marked the beginning of of our Roboprix's journey. The series of challenging events organized include, Line follower race, Maze runner race, Weight class drag race and Obstacle race. All the winners were awarded with cash prize and runner-ups were awarded educational books by our guest of honor, Mr. Arthanarieaswaran S., head of Hardware practices of embedded horizontal BU in L&T on the valedictorian function held on 12th February at 3pm. A heart-warming thank you speech by our faculty coordinator marked the end of our beautiful experience of Roboprix’19.

FAQ

1Are there any prerequisites or skills required to participate in this event?

Everyone is welcome. While experience in hardware domain and fundamental in robotics is a huge plus, teams will also need people with strong presentation skills and brilliant ideas.

2 How long is this event?

RoboPrix 21 is a three days event – 19th MAY 2021 to 21st MAY 2021.

3Do I need a team?

For the Ideathon, only team participation is allowed (max 3 members). Whereas the Robotics quiz event is an individual-only event.

4 How much does it cost?

Robotics quiz - ₹50 Ideathon - ₹100 (per team)

5 Is this an onsite event?

No, keeping present situation in mind because of COVID-19, this is strictly an online event.

6 Is this event only for VIT students?

No, this event is open for students coming for different colleges and schools too.

7 How can I Register?

You can register through our official “RoboPrix” website, where you will be redirected to a link (google form). You are supposed to fill the required details and finally proceed with the payment.

8 Who can participate?

Any budding engineer with passion for hardware-oriented learning can participate in this event.

9 Do I have to stay the whole time?

No, you only need to be present when you’re registered event starts. But we planned for an interactive session at the end, if it interests you in any way contestants are always welcome.

10 What will be the basis of judgement?

The following will be taken into account: The marks allotted to you by the mentors during the reviews. The marks allotted by the judge panel during the final presentation. For the quiz, participant with the highest score at the end is chosen as the winner. The decision of the organising committee would be final and abiding.

11 Can students from different colleges form a team?

Yes, students from different colleges can be part of the same team

12 This is the first time I’m participating in a Ideathon. Are there any pre-requisites?

There are no prerequisites. Just some curiosity and zeal to learn would suffice. We would love to host your first Ideathon!

13 What is the Code of Conduct of RoboPrix 21?

We request all the participants to maintain integrity and professional competence at all times. All participants are requested to show mutual respect. For the smooth conductance of all the events, we request everyone to show their professional behaviour at any cost. Malpractices like plagiarism of ideas are strictly discouraged. Involvement in such activities might result in withdrawal of your registration.
